What we do

Fly to the Limit is a small start up airline offering adventure flights, transport and sight seeing based in the senic capital of New Zealand, therefore the world.

Operating out of Queenstown and Wanaka, FTTL offer tourists a range of scenic flights in both fixed wing and helicopters, as well as high adventure in a stunt plane, or the most peaceful flight experience possible in a glider high above the mountains. They specialise in one-off charters, and delivery and pickup for trampers, also providing a provisioning service for fresh supplies during long excursions.

Our company is aimed at those who are looking for a one-off extreme experience in the most beautiful region of Earth.
Adrenaline pumping aerobatics, peaceful gliding through the mountains, flying in to remote lakes, tramping/climbing expeditions, custom sight seeing by the hour, day or week.
